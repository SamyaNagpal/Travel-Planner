About Shimla

Shimla, the capital of Himachal Pradesh, is famous for its colonial architecture, scenic beauty, and pleasant climate. It was the summer capital of British India.

Top Attractions

The Mall Road

The main shopping street with colonial buildings and cafes

Kufri

Popular for skiing and Himalayan wildlife zoo

Christ Church

Second oldest church in North India with beautiful stained glass

Quick Facts

  • Best Time to Visit: March to June, December-January
  • Average Temperature: 15°C to 30°C (summer), -5°C to 10°C (winter)
  • Famous For: Hill station, colonial architecture, adventure sports
