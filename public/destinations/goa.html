Goa

About Goa

Goa is India's smallest state known for its spectacular beaches, Portuguese heritage, vibrant nightlife and delicious seafood. A former Portuguese colony, it blends Indian and Mediterranean cultures beautifully.

Top Attractions

Calangute Beach

Queen of beaches with golden sands and water sports

Basilica of Bom Jesus

UNESCO site with St. Francis Xavier's relics

Dudhsagar Falls

Majestic four-tiered waterfall in the jungle

Quick Facts

  • Best Time to Visit: November to February
  • Average Temperature: 25°C to 35°C
  • Famous For: Beaches, nightlife, seafood
  • Unique Feature: Only place in India with two-wheel taxis
