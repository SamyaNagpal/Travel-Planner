Safety Essentials for Women Travelers

Items we recommend for every woman traveler regardless of destination

Personal Alarm

A small personal alarm that can emit a loud sound to deter potential threats and attract attention in emergency situations.

First Aid Kit

A compact first aid kit with bandages, antiseptic wipes, pain relievers, and any personal medications you might need.

Door Stop Alarm

A portable door wedge with built-in alarm that can secure your accommodation door and alert you if someone tries to enter.

Power Bank

Always keep your devices charged with a reliable power bank so you can call for help or use navigation when needed.

Modest Clothing Options

Pack at least one modest outfit that covers shoulders and knees for visiting religious sites or conservative areas.

Document Copies

Keep digital and physical copies of your important documents separate from the originals in case of loss or theft.
